By Air

  • Airport shuttle service available

  • Metro line to city center

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the visa requirements for Kazakhstan?

Citizens of many countries can enter Kazakhstan visa-free for up to 30 days. Please check with the Kazakhstan embassy in your country for specific requirements.

Is there accommodation near the venue?

Yes, there are several hotels within 5 km of the venue. We recommend booking early as availability may be limited during the event dates.

What languages are supported at the event?

The event supports Kazakh, Russian, and English. Simultaneous interpretation is available for all main conference sessions.
