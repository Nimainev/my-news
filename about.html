About PowerTechExpo

Since 2020, we’ve been Central Asia’s premier platform for green energy innovation, connecting industry leaders and driving sustainable energy transformation across the region.

Our Mission

To accelerate the adoption of renewable energy and sustainable technologies across Central Asia and CIS countries by creating the premier platform for industry networking, knowledge sharing, and business development.

Our Vision

To be the catalyst for Central Asia’s transition to a sustainable energy future, fostering innovation and collaboration that benefits the entire region.

What You’ll Experience

  • Innovation

    Promoting cutting-edge technologies that drive the green energy transition.

  • Collaboration

    Fostering partnerships between industry leaders, governments, and innovators.

  • Sustainability

    Advancing sustainable energy solutions for a cleaner future.

  • Excellence

    Delivering world-class events that exceed participant expectations.

Our Journey

  • 2020
    PowerTechExpo Founded

    Launched as Central Asia’s first dedicated green energy exhibition.

  • 2021
    First Exhibition

    Hosted 50 exhibitors and 3,000 visitors in Almaty.

  • 2023
    Regional Expansion

    Expanded to include CIS countries with 100+ exhibitors.

  • 2025
    Record Breaking

    Achieved 15,000+ visitors and 150+ exhibitors.

  • 2026
    Future Vision

    Targeting 20,000+ visitors and 200+ exhibitors.
